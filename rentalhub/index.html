Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Simply click "Sign Up Free" and log in with your Google account. No separate registration needed — one click and you're in!

How do I pay for a rental?

After booking, you'll see our GCash number and bank details. Send the payment, take a screenshot, and upload it as proof. Our admin team will verify and confirm your booking within 24 hours.

Can I cancel my booking?

Cancellations depend on the rental item and how far in advance you request. Please contact us before your start date for assistance.

How long does approval take?

Most bookings are reviewed and approved within a few hours during business days. You'll see the updated status in your dashboard.

What if the item is unavailable on my dates?

Our system checks availability. If an item shows as "Unavailable", you can check back later or browse similar items in the same category.

Is this safe and secure?

Yes! All logins are handled through Google. Your personal data is never shared. Payments are verified manually by our admin team.
